Conditions We Treat

Common problems. Uncommonly thorough care.

For each of these, the treatment plan looks different — because the underlying cause is different. The exam tells us which path is right. Here is what we see most often and how we approach it.

Spine

Lower Back Pain

Most lower back pain has a core weakness or hip mobility component that adjustments alone won't fix. We combine spinal work with rehabilitation, decompression for disc-related cases, and corrective exercise so the relief lasts.

Nerve

Sciatica

Sciatica is a symptom, not a diagnosis. We identify the actual source — disc, piriformis, SI joint, or lumbar nerve root — and treat accordingly. Spinal decompression and corrective work are often part of the plan.

Neck & Head

Neck Pain & Migraines

Desk work, phone posture, old whiplash, stress — neck pain rarely has a single cause. Adjustments, postural retraining, soft-tissue work, and acupuncture are often combined for chronic patterns.

Upper Body

Shoulder & Rotator Cuff Pain

Shoulder dysfunction is almost always a postural and biomechanical issue, not just a shoulder issue. Treatment integrates the upper back, scapula, and rotator cuff — with shock wave therapy for chronic tendon cases.

Lower Body

Knee & IT Band Pain

Runners, golfers, and active 50+ patients are the most common cases. Treatment usually addresses hip mechanics and glute strength along with the knee itself. Cold laser and corrective exercise round out the plan.

Performance Injury

Golf Injury & Swing Correction

Dr. Tanner is TPI Certified at Levels 1 and 2 and developed the Golfer's Edge program at Desert Mountain. We treat the injury and assess the swing mechanics that caused it — because if the swing doesn't change, the injury comes back.

Athletics

Sports & Overuse Injuries

Tennis, pickleball, hiking, lifting — overuse injuries respond well to integrated care. Shock wave for stubborn tendinopathy, cold laser for inflammation, corrective exercise to fix the loading pattern that caused it.

Lifestyle

Postural & Desk-Job Pain

Forward head, rounded shoulders, locked-up upper back, weak glutes from sitting. Adjustments restore motion; corrective exercise and Pilates keep it from coming back. Common, fixable.

Recovery

Post-Surgical Recovery

Once your surgeon has cleared you for activity, integrated rehab can accelerate your return. We coordinate with your surgical team and build a graduated return-to-activity plan around the new mechanics of the repair.

If you're considering surgery

Many of our patients used shock wave therapy to avoid the operating room.

Especially for tendon injuries (rotator cuff, plantar fasciitis, tennis elbow, achilles), shock wave therapy has helped patients heal what would otherwise have been surgical cases. We are one of the few chiropractic clinics in the Valley with the equipment.

Our Services

Ten integrated services. One roof. One doctor coordinating them.

Most chiropractic offices offer one tool — the adjustment — and refer everything else out. Tanner Chiropractic keeps the full toolkit under one roof so the care stays coordinated and you don't run from clinic to clinic.

Foundation

Chiropractic Care

Precise spinal and extremity adjustments to restore joint motion and relieve nerve irritation. The starting point of most treatment plans — but rarely the entire plan.

Performance

Sports Injury Treatment

Diagnosis and treatment of athletic injuries with emphasis on returning you to your sport. Dr. Tanner is a Certified Chiropractic Sports Medicine Specialist and has treated professional boxers and PGA pros.

Pain & Recovery

Acupuncture

Effective for chronic pain, migraines, soft-tissue recovery, and tension that doesn't fully resolve with adjustments alone. Used as a complement to mechanical care.

Advanced Modality

Shock Wave Therapy

Acoustic-wave therapy that stimulates healing in tendons, ligaments, and chronic soft-tissue injuries. We are one of the few chiropractic clinics in the Valley offering it — and many patients have used it to avoid surgery.

Disc Care

Spinal Decompression

Targeted traction therapy for herniated, bulging, or degenerated discs in the lumbar or cervical spine. Provides drug-free relief for conditions that often get sent to surgery too early.

Tissue Healing

Cold Laser Therapy

Low-level laser therapy that accelerates cellular repair, reduces inflammation, and relieves pain. Useful for tendinopathies, sprains, and lingering post-injury symptoms.

Movement

Corrective Exercise & Rehabilitation

Targeted strengthening and movement retraining built around your specific imbalances. Dr. Tanner is a Certified Exercise Strength & Conditioning Specialist — this is the "why it doesn't keep coming back" piece.

Core & Posture

Clinical Pilates

Reformer- and mat-based Pilates programmed clinically around your spine, posture, and goals. Excellent for active adults rebuilding core stability after injury or extended desk work.

Soft Tissue

Therapeutic Massage

Deep tissue, sports, and trigger-point work coordinated with your adjustment schedule for maximum recovery. We treat it as treatment, not pampering.

Golfer's Edge

TPI Golf Performance & Injury Prevention

Dr. Tanner is TPI Certified at Levels 1 and 2 and developed the Golfer's Edge program for the Desert Mountain Golf Community. Treatment integrates swing biomechanics with corrective work so the injury and the cause are addressed together.

How It Fits Together

The integrated model — why these all live under one roof.

A typical chiropractor refers out for massage, refers out for acupuncture, refers out for rehab, and never sees the patient's golf swing. We do all of it here. That means the adjustment, the soft tissue, the corrective exercise, and the swing assessment all align — coordinated by one doctor who actually knows what the others did.

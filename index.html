Our Approach

Most chiropractic care fixes the restriction. We fix what caused it.

Adjustments relieve the immediate pain — but they don't correct the muscle imbalances, postural dysfunction, or movement patterns that caused the problem in the first place. That's why most patients come back six months later with the same issue.

Our integrated approach combines chiropractic adjustments with corrective exercise, acupuncture, shock wave therapy, spinal decompression, cold laser, clinical Pilates, and soft-tissue work. Each tool addresses a specific layer of the problem.

The result: faster recovery, longer-lasting relief, and patients who don't need to keep coming back.

Meet Your Chiropractor

Dr. Glen J. Tanner, D.C.

Dr. Tanner has spent 30+ years helping North Scottsdale's active adults, golfers, and athletes move better, recover faster, and stay out of pain. He is a graduate of Palmer College of Chiropractic West and holds post-graduate certification in Sports Rehabilitation and Physiotherapy from the University of Bridgeport.

Over his career he has served as physician and corner man for multiple world champion boxers through Top Rank Boxing (17 years), as physician for PGA professionals, and as the developer of the Golfer's Edge program at Desert Mountain. He has been recognized as one of Arizona's Top 10 Best Chiropractors.

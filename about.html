About Tanner Chiropractic

Dr. Glen J. Tanner, D.C., C.C.S.M.S., TPI

30+ years of clinical experience. Former team physician for world champion boxers and PGA professionals. Trusted by North Scottsdale's most demanding patients.

The Practitioner

A specialist who actually treats specialists.

Dr. Tanner has spent 30+ years helping North Scottsdale's active adults, golfers, and athletes move better, recover faster, and stay out of pain. His patients have ranged from professional boxers and PGA tour players to weekend warriors who refuse to slow down.

His clinical philosophy: most musculoskeletal pain has multiple contributing factors. Lower back pain often stems from core weakness. Shoulder dysfunction from postural compensation. Sciatica from chronic biomechanical imbalance. Adjustments address the immediate restriction — they don't correct the muscle imbalances, postural dysfunction, or movement patterns that created the problem. That is where traditional treatment stops, and where his approach begins.

17 Years · Top Rank Boxing

Team physician for world champions.

For 17 years, Dr. Tanner served as physician and corner man for multiple world champion boxers through Top Rank Boxing. The work demanded the ability to assess and treat under pressure — between rounds, between fights, on the road.

That experience shapes how he treats today. Most patients don't have a heavyweight title on the line — but they do have a season, a tournament, a tee time, a hike, a grandchild they want to lift. The standard of care doesn't change.

Golf · The Golfer's Edge

Built around the swing.

Dr. Tanner is TPI Certified at Level 1 (Golf Fitness Instructor) and Level 2 (Golf Injuries) through the Titleist Performance Institute — the leading credential for golf-specific biomechanics.

He developed the Golfer's Edge performance and injury-prevention program for the Desert Mountain Golf Community, and has served as physician for PGA professionals. Treatment integrates swing analysis with corrective work so the injury and the cause get addressed together.

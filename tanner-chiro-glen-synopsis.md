## The Three Principles Behind The Design

### 1. Subtraction beats addition.

The Upwork developer's revision had 15 sections and 25 photos. Your current live site has 8 nav menus and dozens of sub-pages. **The new site has 9 focused sections and 4 photos.** Less to read. More to remember. Premium feels less crowded.

### 2. Specifics sell. Claims don't.

"30 years experience" is a claim. *"Physician and corner man for multiple world champion boxers. Physician for world-class and professional golfers. Developer of the Golfer's Edge program at Desert Mountain."* — those are specifics. The new site is built on specifics.

### 3. One ask.

Your current site has eight different buttons (Schedule Appointment, Book Now, Call Today, Learn More, Read More, Get Started, etc.). The new site has **one ask, repeated everywhere: "Book Your Consultation."** A confused visitor doesn't decide — they leave. We removed the confusion.
